Provider Directory Consolidation
Consolidates provider and facility network data from three Federal Fiscal Year snapshots (January 1 of each year) into a single searchable deliverable. Sources three historical eVIP Provider Directory files. Classifies each record into the 9 applicable CMS categories (a–j, i omitted) with NPIs and provider names per CMS guidance.
