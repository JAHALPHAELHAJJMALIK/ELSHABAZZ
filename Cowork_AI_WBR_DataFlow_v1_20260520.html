Workflow Purpose
Automated Weekly Business Review (WBR)
Transforms a half-day manual metrics review into a single scheduled slash command (/wbr weekly prep run). Pulls data from 8 connectors, prepares numbers & focus areas Sunday night, enables human narrative steering Monday morning, and ships a polished metrics document + leadership slide. One trust-line sentence inside the prompt ensures source fidelity and prevents hallucination.
