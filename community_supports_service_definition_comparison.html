Remediation: Add WHEN to Auth UPDATE Insert the following WHEN clause into the DIM_AUTHORIZATIONSERVICE CASE (before ELSE NULL):

/*18 - Sobering Centers*/
WHEN (TRIM(f.value) = 'H0014'
  AND CONCAT(UPPER(a.HCPC_MODCODE_SEARCH_1),','
  ,UPPER(a.HCPC_MODCODE_SEARCH_2),','
  ,UPPER(a.HCPC_MODCODE_SEARCH_3))
  LIKE '%U6%')
THEN 'Sobering Centers'
